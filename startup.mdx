# Startup & Installation

<img src="/images/install.svg" alt="Installation" />

<Warning>
Most installs break for two reasons: **renaming the resource** or **starting it late**. Don’t do either.
</Warning>

## Folder structure (required)

Your resources must be placed like this:

```text
server/
  resources/
    [EC]/
      Eagle/
      EC-PANEL/
```

<Note>
`[EC]` is just a category folder. The resource names are **Eagle** and **EC-PANEL**.
</Note>

## Install steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Extract">
    Unzip the download on your machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place resources">
    Move the folders into: `resources/[EC]/`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Do not rename">
    Keep folder names **exactly**: `Eagle` and `EC-PANEL`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ensure order">
    Ensure **Eagle first** in `server.cfg`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup>
```cfg server.cfg (minimum)
ensure Eagle
ensure EC-PANEL
```
```cfg server.cfg (recommended order)
ensure Eagle
ensure EC-PANEL

# framework + DB next
ensure oxmysql
ensure es_extended

# everything else after
ensure [standalone]
ensure [jobs]
ensure [cars]
```
</CodeGroup>
